Welcome to this Sudoku Master project!

Sudoku is a popular Japanese puzzle game, based on the logical placement of numbers.
Your goal is to fill in a 9*9 grid with digits so that each column, row and 3*3 section contain numbers from 1 to 9.
You will choose one of the three difficulties; Easy, Medium or Hard. Your mission is to use logic to fill in the missing digits and complete the grid.
